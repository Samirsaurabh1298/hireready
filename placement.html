Important Terms

Placement Agreement &
Terms & Conditions

💰 Placement Fee

A placement support fee of ₹60,000 is payable only after you receive and accept a job offer through our program. No upfront fee is charged for placement support.

📋 Agreement Requirements

To enroll in the Placement Support program, candidates must submit a signed placement agreement along with a recent photograph. Both are mandatory.

✅ Eligibility

The program is open to freshers and experienced professionals. A valid resume and basic qualifications are required. Our team will assess eligibility after application.

⏱️ Program Duration

Active placement support is provided for up to 6 months. Job alerts and referrals are sent regularly throughout this period.

🔒 Data Privacy

Your personal information, resume, photo, and signature are stored securely and used solely for placement purposes. We never share your data without consent.

❌ Refund Policy

Since placement fee is post-placement, no refunds are applicable. For other services (resume, LinkedIn), refunds are considered within 48 hours of purchase if no work has started.

⚠️ By submitting the application form and uploading your signature, you agree to the HireReady Placement Support Agreement. Please read all terms carefully before applying.
